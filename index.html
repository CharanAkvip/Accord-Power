Accord Power Conversion Pvt. Ltd established in 2012, Specializes in manufacturing Electric Vehicle Chargers for 2-wheelers, 3-wheelers, and 4-wheelers, along with power supplies for a wide range of applications, including Electric Vehicles, Water Purifiers, Set-Top Boxes, Laptops, office automation, and more. Since our inception, we have meticulously developed state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and implemented rigorous quality systems aligned with international product and quality standards.

Accord’s products have received strong market acceptance, serving diverse sectors such as Electric Vehicles, Telecom, Water Purification, Set-Top Boxes, Laptops, and beyond. Our unwavering commitment to quality and industry compliance has established Accord Power Conversion as a trusted and reliable name in the power supply and EV charger manufacturing industry.
