Company Directors

Adla Venkat Reddy

Managing Director
venkatreddy@accordpower.in

Mr. Adla Venkat Reddy, with 30 years of manufacturing experience, founded Accord Power Conversion in 2012. Under his visionary leadership, the company has expanded to three units, establishing itself as a leading brand in energy conversion. Coming from a modest agricultural background, he is committed to driving development and creating rural employment. His strategic approach, streamlined processes, and automation have fueled Accord’s rapid growth, making him an inspiration for aspiring entrepreneurs.

Appidi Sampath Reddy

Technical Director
sampath@accordpower.in

With over 22 years of industry experience, Sampath Reddy is a key technical leader, driving innovation and reliability at Accord Power Conversion. His expertise in R&D has led to groundbreaking products with national and international certifications like CE, BIS, and BS. Renowned for his problem-solving skills and customer-centric approach, he has revolutionized production processes, fueling the company’s growth and industry leadership. His dedication to excellence continues to shape the organization’s success in a dynamic market.

Sridhar Reddy

Operations Director
sridhar@accordpower.in

Sridhar Reddy, the youngest director at Accord, brings a global perspective with a master's in Industrial Engineering from the US and experience as a business analyst. Upon returning to India, he spearheaded the establishment of Unit 2, specializing in SMPS products for industries like RO water purifiers, IT, and telecom. His strategic leadership, innovation, and expertise in material sourcing have driven Unit 2’s rapid growth, positioning it as a key industry player delivering high-quality, cutting-edge solutions.

Gaurav Saini

Managing Partner
gaurav@accordpower.in

Gaurav Saini brings dynamic energy and entrepreneurial expertise to Accord, driving marketing and business development. As Managing Partner of Accord Power Digital Products, he established the Control Panel division and led impactful marketing campaigns. Representing Accord at national and international expos, he showcases its innovations globally, playing a key role in the company’s growth and success.
