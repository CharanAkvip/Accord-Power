Milestones

Accord Power Conversion Pvt. Ltd. is committed to delivering innovative, high-quality power solutions with a focus on sustainability and technological excellence.

Marking the genesis of its journey, Accord embarked on crafting adapters for water purifying & telecom applications 5000 sft area.

Accord gets ISO 9001:2008 certified, a testament to its commitment to excellence.

Accord becomes the preferred vendor for " the top 10 OEMs, solidifying its position within the industry ".

Initiated APDP towards producing products tailored for commercial water plants.

Accord becomes the first " Indian company to secure approval for over 50 products from BIS, a testament to its unwavering dedication to quality ".

The company expanded by " Establishing a state of the art facility, encompassing " an additional 20,000 sft area dedicated to Adapters and separate division for EV products.

EV unit transitioned to a grand 50,000 sft space, achieving approval from ARAI & ICAT for its line of products, making a milestone in innovation & compliance.

Development of a fully automatic SMT facility, enabling the production of 1.0L EV Chargers per month, underscoring the company's commitment to cutting-edge manufacturing.

Manufacturing facility added another 40,000 sft with this 1 million adaptors and charger can be produced.

Mission, Vision & Values

Accord Power Conversion Pvt. Ltd. is committed to delivering innovative, high-quality power solutions with a focus on sustainability and technological excellence.
